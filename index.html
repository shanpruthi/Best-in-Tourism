What makes BiT so powerful?

BiT uses the best of Twitter's streaming and REST APIs, in association with NodeJS and FireBase to create an optimal environment for users to see the most exciting attractions in real time, as well as the most popular locations in the past few hours (based on favourites, retweets, and tweets).
